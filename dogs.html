Why chose dogs as pet for your family?

It's no surprise that dogs are the most common pet in the world. They are cute, fun, loving, playful, and a man's best friend. Movies such as Airbud, Hachi, 8 below showcase the companionship of dogs and thier owners. Below you will find several breeds of dogs and some highlighting factors.

Golden Doodle Dogs

  • Height: 20-24 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 10-15 years
  • black,blonde,rust colored hypoallegenic dog!

Wiener Dogs

  • Height: 5-9 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 12-16 years
  • more commonly known as weiner dog, super friendly fun dogs!

Golden Retriver Dogs

  • Height: 21-24 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 10-12 years
  • Golden Colored physically active family dogs!

Pug Dogs

  • Height: 10-13 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 13-15 years
  • family friendly loving dog!
