Are cats or dogs the best pet?

Pets

The age old fight of cats and dogs, which pet is better? This site gives you some of the most popular breeds of cats and dogs, and a little information about each of them. Take your pick!

Golden Doodle

  • Height: 20-24 inches tall.
  • fLife expectancy: 10-15 years
  • black,blonde,rust colored hypoallegenic dog!

Dachshund Dog

  • Height: 5-9 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 12-16 years
  • more commonly known as weiner dog, super friendly fun dogs!

Golden Retriver

  • Height: 21-24 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 10-12 years
  • Golden Colored physically active family dogs!

Pug

  • Height: 10-13 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 13-15 years
  • family friendly loving dog!

Persian Cat

  • Height: 10-15 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 10-15 years
  • Friendy cat, requires grooming, snuggly!

Sphynx

  • Height: 8-10 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 9-15 years
  • Highly intelligent, nonshedding, hairless!

Ragdoll Cat

  • Height: 9-11 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 13-18 years
  • Highyl Intelligent, fluffy, easily groomed!

Exotic Shorthair Cat

  • Height: 8-15 inches tall.
  • Life expectancy: 13-15 years
  • sociable, short coat, quiet, and a little bit active!
